About IJAPB

International Journal of Advances in Pharmacy and Biotechnology (IJAPB) is a broad spectrum open access journal that publishes articles quarterly. The journal publishes research articles, review articles, short communications and case studies related to:
Pharmaceutics,
Pharmacology,
Toxicology,
Pharmacognosy,
Pharmaceutical Chemistry,
Pharmaceutical Analysis,
Quality Assurance,
Regulatory Affairs,
Pharmaceutical Technology,
Biopharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics,
Pharmacy Practice,
Alternative Medicine and
Biotechnology.
Authors should prepare their manuscripts according to specifications given by IJAPB. Failure to follow them may result in papers being delayed or rejected. All papers are subjected to peer-review before accepting or asking for revision or rejection.
IJAPB is an international door to knowledge-sharing. This journal provides the researchers, scientists, academicians, industrialists, students an ample scope to publish and excel in the fields of Biotechnology and Pharmaceutical Sciences.
Copyright
Submission of the manuscript represent that the manuscript has not been published previously and is not considered for publication elsewhere. Corresponding author would be required to sign a Copy Right Transfer Form once the manuscript is accepted. The corresponding author can download the form, fill it, sign it and can be sent as an attachment file through email or by post/courier along with the publication charges.
Open Access Policy
IJAPB is committed to provide open access to all it publications as pdf files for all readers to encourage sharing of knowledge. Readers can download the published articles any number of times and there will be no charges for downloading.
